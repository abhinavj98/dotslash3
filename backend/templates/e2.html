Step 1 : With a dumbbell in each hand, palms facing in, stand upright with your feet shoulder width apart and your arms down by your sides. This is your starting position.

Step 2 : With straight arms, exhale as you raise the dumbbells out to the sides until your arms are parallel with the floor.

Step 3 : Hold the contraction for a moment and then inhale as you lower the dumbbells back to your starting position.

Step 4 : Repeat for a full set.
