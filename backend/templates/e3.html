Step 1 : Grasp a barbell with hands wider than shoulder width apart and your palms facing down.

Step 2 : Keep your feet shoulder width apart and your knees slightly bent.

Step 3 : Raise the barbell to your chest and then overhead and slightly forward for your starting position.

Step 4 : Inhale as you slowly lower the bar back to your chest.

Step 5 : Exhale as you slowly raise the bar back overhead to the starting position.

Step 6 : Repeat for a complete set.
